Think About A Child

Think About a Child Organization is a 501(c)(3) charitable nonprofit organization with a primary mission provide a spiritual, social, and moral support toward decreasing juvenile delinquency because today’s children are tomorrow’s adults. We intend to work primary in Haiti, ultimately expanding to serve other countries in dire need as we acquired the resources necessary to help.

We aim to contribute the long-term sustainability of Haiti by establishing schools and training centers. Our organization leads by example, creating a united front in Haiti and that fosters a climate of growth and personal development for the inhabitants.

Our current primary pursuit is establishing a facility in the south of Haiti that will serve as facility for our target population meeting the needs of shelter, education, nutrition, worship, healthcare, and agriculture throughout Haiti.
